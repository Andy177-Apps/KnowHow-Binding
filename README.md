# KnowHow-Binding


An app that connects people with knowledge. For anyone looking for a tutor, student, exchanging skills or grouping a book club, you can find your favorite friend on Know How Binding!

<img src="https://upload.cc/i1/2021/06/25/4j5qBv.png" width="500" />


## Features

### Article
* Posting articles to Let interested friends can contact you with expected form: Student, Tutor, Skill Exchange or Study Group.
* The search bar at the top of the screen allows users to quickly find information they are interested in

<img src=https://upload.cc/i1/2021/06/25/v9nKAx.png height="500" /> 



### Discover
* Quickly list search results based on the criteria you choose
<img src=https://upload.cc/i1/2021/06/25/Z9SL36.png height="500" /> 



### Calendar

* Calendar function for user to both be noted or reminded of an appointment you created with your learning partner and launch a activity with your following users

<img src=https://upload.cc/i1/2021/06/25/rPal7O.png height="500" /> 


### Message

* Chat room allowing user to tart a conversation with someone who is potential users

<img src=https://upload.cc/i1/2021/06/25/k2ILlC.png height="500" /> 


### Profile

* Recommend members based on user's profile
 <img src=https://upload.cc/i1/2021/06/25/aqfzVi.png height="500" /> 

* Upload a picture to the avatar or background picture to show the user's personal characteristics
 <img src=https://upload.cc/i1/2021/06/25/LXq9yr.png height="500" /> 


## Implementation
* **Design Pattern**: `MVVM`, `OOP`, `Observer`, `Factory`
* **Jetpack:** `Data Bindng`, `Navigation`, `ViewModel`, `LiveData`, `Permissions`, `WorkManager`
* **Firebase:** `Cloud Firestore`, `Crashlytics`, `Google Login API`, `Authentication`
* **User Interface**: `RecyclerView`, `Waterfall`, `TimePicker`, `TabLayout`, `Chip`, `RadioGroup`, `FloatingActionButton`
